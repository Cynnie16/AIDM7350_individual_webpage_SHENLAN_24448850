We Are —— 🥇 Chinese National Table Tennis Team 🥇

"The Road To The Champion Is Never Smooth"

Since its establishment in 1952, the national table tennis team has been playing on the courts, and has perfected the skills of the small silver ball through thousands of trials and tribulations, and has performed superbly, which has also earned table tennis the reputation of "the national ball". Behind the remarkable achievements is the spirit of hard work and continuous struggle of generations of national table tennis players, which has become the team's soul, lasting forever, and turned into action, shining on the court. Here are the main team members now.👇🏼👇🏼👇🏼
